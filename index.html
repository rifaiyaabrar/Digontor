About Us

Bangladeshi schoolteachers rarely receive proper teacher development and training opportunities, especially teachers teaching in remote cities and rural areas. They often have no other resources to reinforce their teaching apart from the national curriculum and textbooks. This application aims to support Bangladeshi schoolteachers of English by providing them with necessary resources including lesson plans, additional materials, tutorials on using digital tools and resources through an android application that they will be able to use both online and offline as well as a website for desktop access. There are also sections that will give updates regarding conferences and publication opportunities. There is also a section named “blogs” which can be used by teachers to provide feedback to us.

This android application paired with a website aims to help English teachers make their classrooms more interactive. Initially, teachers are provided with interaction-based lesson plans and encouraged to use them in their classroom, thus helping them to move away from the traditional practice of conducting lecture-based language classes. These lesson plans and materials are open to adjustments, as a result, teachers will be able to use their autonomy and own ideas to adapt the resources according to the necessities of their contexts. However, teachers are also requested to create their own lesson plans and materials and send them to us, so that they can be uploaded on the website and the application to allow their fellow teachers have access to them. There are three basic criteria for a lesson plan uploaded, namely, 1) the lesson plans are based on the recent national curriculum and textbook contents, 2) the lesson plans are student-centred and promote student interaction in class, and 3) the lesson plans are easily executable, even in low-resource contexts. We hope this application helps you in one way or the other in your teaching!
